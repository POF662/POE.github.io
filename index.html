Welcome to Proof-of-Fitness

Proof-of-Fitness (PoF) is a blockchain-based fitness rewards platform that incentivizes healthy living by turning physical activities into valuable tokens. With PoF, every workout, run, or cycle can be turned into rewards that benefit you in the real world.

How It Works

  • Register: Sign up and connect your favorite fitness apps like Google Fit or Strava.
  • Track: Our platform automatically tracks your fitness activities.
  • Earn PoF Tokens: Receive PoF tokens as rewards for completing activities.
  • Redeem & Enjoy: Use your tokens to redeem fitness products, premium memberships, or even stake for higher rewards!

Token Utility & Revenue Model

PoF tokens aren’t just for show—they have real utility within the ecosystem:

  • Transaction Fees: A 3-5% fee is applied during token redemptions.
  • Premium Memberships: Unlock premium features, exclusive challenges, and more by staking PoF tokens.
  • Marketplace Commissions: Vendors pay a small fee to list fitness products on the platform.
  • Sponsored Challenges: Fitness brands can sponsor exclusive challenges, paying a fee in PoF or fiat for listing.

Roadmap

Our journey to revolutionize fitness:

  1. Phase 1: Launch the PoF token on PinkSale and build a strong community.
  2. Phase 2: Develop an MVP with Google Fit integration and a basic dashboard.
  3. Phase 3: Introduce a fitness product marketplace and premium features.
  4. Phase 4: Expand into a full-fledged platform supporting multiple fitness apps and community governance.
