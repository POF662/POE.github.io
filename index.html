About the Project

Proof-of-Fitness aims to bridge the gap between blockchain technology and the fitness community by creating a transparent, rewards-based ecosystem. Users will be able to connect their fitness apps, track activities, and earn PoF tokens, which can be redeemed for fitness gear, premium memberships, and more. The ultimate goal is to create a community-driven platform where fitness is not only beneficial but also rewarding.

We are in the **conceptual phase** and are looking to raise $35,000 - $50,000 to fund our MVP development. The MVP will initially focus on Google Fit and Strava integrations and a basic rewards dashboard.
